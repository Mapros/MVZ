Contraceptive Campaign








My Voice in Zimbabwe is the host of the I PLAN campaign for family planning which is designed to develop a young person with knowledge, skills and attitudes to identify possible family planning methods to use as for all ages including a method safe for adolescent girls and young adolescent mothers.This campaign targets young adolescent girls and mothers from Key and Vulnerable Population, Young People Living with HIV group and Young People in Tertiary Education Institutions. The campaign program phase last for 3 months and expect to meet objectives of :

•To identify different family planning methods.

•Know the correct and basic knowledge of a desired family planning of choice.

•Understand the linkage of family planning with menstruation and hygiene.
